# Privacy Policy for RethinkSoft in Chrome

Last updated: February 1, 2026

## Overview
RethinkSoft in Chrome is a browser automation extension that uses AI to help users complete web tasks.

## Data Collection
This extension:
- Does NOT collect or store personal data on external servers
- Does NOT track browsing history
- Does NOT sell or share user data with third parties

## Data Processing
- **Screenshots and page content** are sent to your chosen AI provider (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, or OpenRouter) to process automation requests
- **API keys and credentials** are stored locally in Chrome's secure storage
- **Conversation history** is stored locally and can be cleared at any time

## Third-Party Services
When you use this extension, data is sent to your selected AI provider according to their respective privacy policies:
- Anthropic: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy
- OpenAI: https://openai.com/privacy
- Google: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- OpenRouter: https://openrouter.ai/privacy

## Permissions
This extension requires broad permissions (`<all_urls>`) to:
- Read page content for AI understanding
- Take screenshots for visual analysis
- Interact with page elements (click, type, scroll)
- Manage browser tabs

These permissions are used solely for browser automation at your request.
